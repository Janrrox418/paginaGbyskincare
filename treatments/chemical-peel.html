Chrysalis™ Peel System

At Gbyskincare, we’re proud to offer the Chrysalis™ Peel System — a next-generation treatment designed to reveal brighter, smoother, and healthier skin. This professional peel combines advanced science with gentle techniques, giving you visible results with minimal downtime.

What Is the Chrysalis™ Peel?

It's a customized skin-renewal treatment using a blend of mandelic, glycolic, salicylic, and hyaluronic acids to:

  • Clear away dull, damaged skin cells.
  • Hydrate and plump the skin from within.
  • Minimize pores and smooth uneven texture.
  • Brighten skin tone and soften fine lines.

Benefits You Can Expect

After a series of treatments, clients often notice:

  • A radiant, youthful glow.
  • Clearer, smoother skin with reduced breakouts.
  • Firmer, plumper skin with improved elasticity.
  • Even skin tone with fewer dark spots.

Who Is It For?

The Chrysalis™ Peel is suitable for many skin types and concerns, including acne, sun damage, uneven texture, and early signs of aging.

Gentle Yet Effective

Unlike harsh peels, the Chrysalis™ Peel is designed to be effective without aggressive downtime. Most clients experience only mild redness that fades quickly, allowing them to return to daily life with a fresh, glowing complexion.

Your Glow Awaits

At Gbyskincare, we personalize every Chrysalis™ Peel to your unique skin goals. Whether you’re looking to smooth texture, reduce blemishes, or simply refresh your glow — this treatment can help you feel more confident in your skin.
