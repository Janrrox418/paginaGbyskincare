Dermaplaning

Dermaplaning is a highly effective exfoliation technique that unveils a smoother, radiant complexion. This non-invasive procedure involves a trained skincare specialist gently gliding a sterile, surgical scalpel across the surface of the skin to remove dead skin cells and fine vellus hairs (commonly known as "peach fuzz").

The result is remarkably soft, even-textured skin that enhances the absorption of skincare products, creates a flawless base for makeup, and leaves your face looking rejuvenated and luminous.
