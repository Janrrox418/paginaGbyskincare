Dermaplaning

Dermaplaning a highly effective exfoliation technique, is a skincare secret that unveils a smoother, radiant complexion. This non-invasive procedure involves a trained skincare specialist gently gliding a sterile, surgical scalpel across the surface of the skin to remove dead skin cells and fine vellus hairs, often referred to as "peach fuzz." The result is remarkably soft, even-textured skin that not only enhances the absorption of skincare products but also leaves your face looking rejuvenated and more luminous.
