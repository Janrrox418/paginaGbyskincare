HydraFacial®

A Hydrafacial is a skincare treatment that combines cleansing, exfoliation, impurity extraction, hydration, and antioxidant protection, utilizing vortex suction technology and various tips to customize the treatment. It is a non-invasive, non-surgical procedure that seeks to improve the appearance of the skin, leaving it clearer, more hydrated, brighter, and with a smoother texture.

This non-invasive treatment improves skin tone, reduces fine lines, and enhances hydration for an instant glow. Suitable for all skin types, Hydrafacial is gentle yet effective, providing long-lasting results.

HydraFacial® Options

Choose the experience that best suits your skin goals.

Signature HydraFacial

Who is it for? Ideal for first-time clients or those seeking a quick refresh.

How does it work? This foundational treatment deeply cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates the skin using patented vortex technology. It leaves your skin smooth, radiant, and ready to glow.

Deluxe HydraFacial

Who is it for? Perfect for those targeting specific skin concerns like fine lines, dark spots, or acne.

How does it work? Includes all steps of the Signature plus a personalized Booster and LED Light Therapy. Red light boosts collagen; blue light calms acne-prone skin.

Luxe HydraFacial

Who is it for? Designed for clients seeking the ultimate rejuvenation and sculpted glow.

How does it work? Combines all Deluxe features with Lymphatic Drainage Massage to detoxify, reduce puffiness, and enhance circulation. It’s the most comprehensive HydraFacial experience.

Back HydraFacial

Who is it for? Ideal for clients struggling with back acne, congestion, or uneven texture.

How does it work? This treatment uses Vortex Fusion Technology to cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate the back area. It removes impurities, dissolves excess oil, and rejuvenates the skin for a smoother, clearer appearance.

Express HydraFacial

Who is it for? Perfect for busy individuals seeking a quick refresh with visible results.

How does it work? In just three steps—cleanse, extract, and hydrate—this fast treatment delivers smoother, brighter, and deeply nourished skin. It’s the Signature HydraFacial in a shorter format, ideal for monthly maintenance.
