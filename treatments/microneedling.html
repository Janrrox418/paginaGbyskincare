Microneedling

Collagen Induction Therapy

Microneedling, a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure, has gained prominence in recent years as an effective method for rejuvenating the skin. This treatment, also known as collagen induction therapy, involves the use of a specialized device equipped with fine needles that create controlled micro-injuries in the skin's surface. These tiny punctures stimulate the skin's natural healing processes, triggering collagen and elastin production.

As a result, microneedling is celebrated for its ability to improve skin texture, reduce the appearance of scars, fine lines, and wrinkles, and enhance the overall complexion. This procedure is safe, effective, and tailored to each patient's specific needs.

  • Reduces acne scars
  • Improves elasticity and firmness
  • Evens skin tone
  • Fast and minimally invasive treatment

Is microneedling effective? Absolutely. This treatment is clinically proven to stimulate collagen and elastin production through controlled micro-injuries. As illustrated in the comparative diagram, microneedling breaks down old collagen strands and activates blood vessels in the dermis layer. Over time, the skin becomes smoother, thicker, and more resilient.

The procedure is especially effective for reducing fine lines, acne scars, and pigmentation irregularities. It also enhances the absorption of topical treatments and can be safely combined with PRP (platelet-rich plasma) for even greater anti-aging results.

  • Stimulates collagen and elastin naturally
  • Improves skin structure and tone
  • Safe for most skin types, including darker tones
  • Can support hair regrowth and reduce pore size
