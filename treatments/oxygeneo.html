Oxygeneo

Oxygenate + Exfoliate + Nourish

The oxygen facial has been called "the super facial" because it uses patented non-invasive technology to nourish skin from the inside out. It is safe for all skin types and tones and can treat sensitive skin without any irritation. The oxygen facial will instantly brighten the appearance of skin after a single treatment which is why it is a favourite of A-list celebrities before red carpet events. The unique benefit of this treatment is that it can be customized to each individual's skin needs.

It's safe, non-invasive, and suitable for all skin types. Results include improved hydration, reduced fine lines, and a glowing, refreshed complexion after just one session.
