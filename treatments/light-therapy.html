Light Therapy

LED Phototherapy for Skin Wellness

Light Therapy (LED phototherapy) is a non-invasive treatment that uses specific wavelengths of light to improve skin health and appearance. Depending on the color of the light used, different skin concerns can be treated safely and effectively — all without pain, needles, or downtime.

LED therapy stimulates natural cellular processes, enhances collagen production, reduces inflammation, and helps balance the skin. It is safe for all skin types and can be used as a stand-alone treatment or in combination with other cosmetic procedures for optimal results.

Types of LED Light & Benefits

  • Red light: boosts collagen production, reduces fine lines and wrinkles, and improves firmness.
  • Blue light: targets acne-causing bacteria, reduces breakouts and calms inflammation.
  • Green light: helps fade pigmentation, sunspots, and discoloration.
  • Yellow light: soothes sensitive skin, stimulates circulation, and restores radiance.

Beyond cosmetic benefits, LED light therapy can also accelerate healing after other treatments and promote faster recovery of the skin barrier.

Why Choose Light Therapy?

  • Completely non-invasive and painless
  • Safe for all skin types
  • No downtime required
  • Progressive, natural-looking results
  • Improves skin tone, texture, and overall radiance

LED Light Therapy for Acne

Blue LED light is especially effective in treating acne because it penetrates the skin at a depth that targets and eliminates Propionibacterium acnes, the bacteria responsible for breakouts.

By destroying these bacteria and reducing inflammation, LED therapy helps clear active acne, prevents future breakouts, and calms redness without damaging the skin. This makes it an excellent choice for both teenagers and adults struggling with mild to moderate acne.

When combined with red LED light, the treatment not only fights bacteria but also supports skin healing, reduces post-acne marks, and helps restore balance to irritated skin.
