Scalp Treatments

Refresh Your Scalp And Promote Lasting Hair Health.

Scalp care can be achieved through targeted treatments that cleanse, nourish, and rebalance the skin at the root of your hair. Keravive by HydraFacial®

Scalp Health Meets Hair Confidence.

Keravive is a revolutionary scalp treatment designed to cleanse, exfoliate, and nourish the scalp for healthier, fuller-looking hair. Using HydraFacial’s patented Vortex Fusion technology, Keravive removes impurities and stimulates circulation while infusing the scalp with a proprietary blend of growth factors and peptides.

The treatment follows a 3-step protocol: Purify – Light suction and exfoliation open follicles and remove buildup.
Nourish – Hydrating peptides are infused to rebalance the scalp microbiome.
Boost – A take-home peptide spray supports ongoing hair growth and shine.

After just one session, 75% of clients report improved hair fullness, and 82% notice shinier, healthier strands.

Keravive Study Summary

A 5-month study involving 44 adults with hair thinning and recession examined the effects of a Keravive treatment protocol.

The Protocol:

  • Patients received three in-office Keravive treatments, once per month.
  • The treatment involved a cleansing step with Hydrafacial's Extract® Solution and a nourishing step with the Keravive Peptide Complex serum.
  • Patients used a Keravive Peptide Complex take-home spray daily for 5 months.

Key Results:

Investigator-Reported Improvements: At the final check-up, investigators noted a statistically significant reduction in hair thinning, temporal line recession, and an improvement in overall hair appearance.

Patient-Reported Improvements: Participants reported a decrease in bothersome scalp symptoms like itchiness and dryness.

  • 53% reported progressive improvement in hair health.
  • 69% reported progressive improvement in hair fullness.
  • 82% said their hair looked healthier, shinier, and had fewer gray strands.
  • 64% reported reduced hair loss, and 49% noted increased hair growth.
