SCULPTRA® Filler

Stimulate Your Skin’s Natural Collagen

Sculptra® is a unique injectable treatment that works differently from traditional fillers. Instead of simply filling lines or adding immediate volume, it stimulates your skin’s natural collagen production over time. This helps restore structure, elasticity, and firmness gradually for results that can last up to two years.

It’s ideal for patients who want a subtle, natural rejuvenation with progressive improvement. Sculptra® is commonly used to treat facial volume loss, hollow cheeks, deep folds, and overall skin texture, giving a refreshed and youthful appearance without looking “overdone”.
